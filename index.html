Call for Papers

We invite original research contributions on all aspects related to the theme of this workshop. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

Technical Topics

  • Integrated sensing and communications (ISAC)
  • Near-field communications and sensing
  • AI-native air interface and network optimization
  • Generative AI and foundation models for wireless systems
  • Channel estimation, beam training, and CSI feedback
  • Joint communication, sensing, localization, and control
  • Waveform and transceiver design for 6G

Application Topics

  • Autonomous systems and robotics
  • Vehicular and aerial wireless networks
  • Digital twins and environment-aware communications
  • Edge intelligence and distributed learning
  • Energy-efficient and sustainable AI-enabled wireless
  • Testbeds, prototypes, and experimental validation
  • Standardization and practical deployment issues
